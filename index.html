Why this page?

Approaching and disseminating fundamental truths as a collective is imperative and will lead to tremendous future good.

My hope is that this type of page will approximate and facilitate such a future in which you, intelligent agents, can derive utility from my thoughts directly recorded here.
From my perspective, the first step in creating this future is to make my thought streams as open and machine parseable as possible. This page is a working attempt of this. :)
