**Effective Date:** May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Blake Edwards ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our SMS messaging program.

## Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you opt in to receive SMS messages from us:

- Your mobile phone number
- Your name (if voluntarily provided)
- Records of your consent to receive messages, including the date, time, and method of opt-in
- Message delivery and engagement data (e.g., whether a message was delivered or replied to)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

- Send the SMS messages you have opted in to receive
- Respond to your replies and support requests
- Maintain records of consent and opt-out requests as required by applicable law and carrier guidelines
- Comply with legal obligations

## How We Share Your Information

**We do not share, sell, rent, or lease your personal information — including your mobile phone number — to any third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.**

Your information may only be disclosed to:

- Service providers that help us deliver SMS messages (such as Twilio), strictly for the purpose of operating the messaging program
- Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of personal information exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

## Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can reply **STOP** at any time to opt out, or **HELP** for assistance.

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you remain opted in to our messaging program, and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal and regulatory record-keeping obligations.

## Your Rights

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP** to any message you receive. You may request information about the data we hold by contacting us at the address below.

## Children's Privacy

Our messaging program is not intended for individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
